Tips for Finding a Job

Only use trusted sites (exp. www.indeed.com) vs. sketchy sites (exp. www.thisisascamsite.com)

Never give sensitive information in untrusted sites

  • Social Security
  • Bank Account
  • Home address
  • Etc.

Find a job that you will enjoy doing

Expand your job search

Provide a professional and presentable resume

Don't be afraid to ask questions! You want to know what you're getting into before its too late

Most importantly, always stay optimistic, and keep looking
